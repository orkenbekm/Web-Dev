Travel to New york

New York City includes five administrative districts (boroughs ) : the Bronx , Brooklyn , Queens , Manhattan , and Staten Island . The main attractions are located in the borough of Manhattan. Among them are: historic skyscrapers ( the Empire State Building , the Chrysler Building ), Rockefeller Center , the Woolworth Building , the Metropolitan Museum of Art , the Metropolitan Opera , Carnegie Hall , the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum (painting), the American Museum of Natural History (dinosaur skeletons and planetarium), the Plaza Hotel , the Waldorf-Astoria Hotel , the Chelsea Hotel , the UN headquarters , and Harlem .
